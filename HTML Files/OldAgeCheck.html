Types of homes

Apartments

Lady Allum offers a choice of one, two and two with flexible third room apartments to suit a range of different needs. Whether you like to entertain, or simply enjoy your own space, you’ll love the relaxed feel and open-plan design at Lady Allum.

With only quality finishes and a tasteful design, each apartment is well-considered for convenient retirement living.  Relish the peace and comfort all year round with mod-cons including double glazing, a heat pump, heated towel rails, easy-access showers and secure swipe access. Each apartment also features a balcony or patio.

Care Suites

Brand new care suites now available, offering Rest Home and Hospital level care.

All Care Suites have a heat pump and full en-suite, as well as a kitchenette with a fridge and a kettle – perfect for making a cuppa when friends and family come to visit.

You own the right to occupy your Care Suite, so you can personalise it with your favourite furniture and treasured pieces. A DHB assessment is not required for care that is paid for privately.

 

Care Rooms

Your room is your own private space, so you can decorate it with your favourite pictures and ornaments. Standard rooms are large enough for a single bed, a chair and a wardrobe or chest of drawers. The cost of a standard room is included in the Care Services Fee set by the DHB. Premium rooms have additional features such as more space, an ensuite bathroom or outdoor access, but you’ll need to pay an extra amount per day to cover the cost of these premium features.

Couples Care Suites

A Couples Care Suite offers the same assistance and comfort as an individual suite, but with the reassurance of having a loved one right by your side.

With a more spacious layout, Couples Care Suites are designed to keep you together; offering Rest Home and Hospital level care to suit both of your ongoing individual needs*.

With your one-bedroom suite, you’ll enjoy a private ensuite, separate living area and very own kitchenette. But feel free to bring along your own home comforts to really make the space your own.

*Specialist dementia care (secure dementia care or psychogeriatric care) is not available in a Couples Care Suite.
